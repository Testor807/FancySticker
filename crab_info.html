    创作人简介:何翀（1807—1883），字丹山。南海九江烟桥（今属广东佛山）人，居广州河南旧蒙圣里。作画法华嵒，笔致高秀、静逸怡人。 He Chong (1807—1883), style name Danshan, was born in Nanhai County but he lived in Guangzhou. He imitated the style of Hua Yan with an elegant and tranquil approach.
