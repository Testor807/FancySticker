作品名:《杞菊猫蝶图》Chinese Wolfberry, Chrysanthemum, Cat and Butterfly
创作人:孙克弘 Sun Kehong (Ming Dynasty)
创作年份:
创作人简介:孙克弘（1532—1611），字允执，号雪居。华亭（今上海松江）人。擅画花鸟、竹石，中年多作设色，工整清丽；晚年风格放逸，笔法简练。
Sun Kehong (1532—1611), style name Yunzhi, pseudonym Xueju, was born in Huating (present Songjiang of Shanghai). Apt at bird-and-flower, bamboo and rock, he did a number of works with colours in his middle age with a meticulous style. In his late years, he applied concise brushstrokes with an unrestrained approach.
款识: 坐醒群蝶狎，庭除亦晏如。余威钤鼠辈，不患食无鱼。
